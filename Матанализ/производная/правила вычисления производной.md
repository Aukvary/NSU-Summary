$u = \varphi(x), v = \psi(x), c = const$ 

#### 1. $(cu)^\prime = cu^\prime$
- $\Delta x + x \to u + \Delta u \to y + \Delta y = c(u + \Delta u) \to c\Delta u$ 
- $$\underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim }\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = c\underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim}\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = cu^\prime$$
#### 2. $y = u \pm v$ 
- $x + \Delta x \to u + \Delta u, v \pm \Delta v, y + \Delta y$ 
- $y + \Delta y = (u + \Delta u) \pm (v \pm \Delta v) \to \Delta y = \Delta u \pm \Delta v \to \underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim}\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim} \frac{\Delta u}{\Delta x} \pm \underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim} \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta x}$ 
- $(u \pm v)^\prime = u^\prime \pm v^\prime$ 
#### 3. $y = uv$ 
- $y + \Delta y = (u + \Delta u)(v + \Delta v) = uv + \Delta vu + \Delta uv + \Delta u\Delta v$ 
- $\Delta y = u\Delta v + \Delta u v + \underset{\to 0}{\Delta u \Delta v}$ 
- $\underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = u \underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim} \frac{\Delta v}{\delta x} + v \underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim}\frac{\Delta u}{\Delta x}$ 
- $(uv)^\prime = u^\prime v + uv^\prime$ 
#### 4. $y = \frac{u}{v}$ 
- $$y + \Delta y = \frac{u + \Delta u}{v + \Delta v}$$
- $$\Delta = \frac{u + \Delta u}{v + \Delta v} - \frac{u}{v} = \frac{v(u + \Delta u) - u(v + \Delta v)}{v(v +\Delta v)} = \frac{v\Delta u - u\Delta v}{v(v + \Delta v)} = \underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim}\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{\underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim}\frac{\Delta u}{\Delta x}v - u\underset{\Delta x \to 0}{\lim}\frac{\Delta v}{\Delta x}}{v(v + \underset{\to 0}{\Delta v})}$$
- $$(\frac{u}{v})^\prime = \frac{vu^\prime - uv^\prime}{v^2}$$
#### 5. $y = f(\varphi(x))$ 
- $[f(\varphi(x))]^\prime = f^\prime(\varphi(x)) * \varphi^\prime(x)$
