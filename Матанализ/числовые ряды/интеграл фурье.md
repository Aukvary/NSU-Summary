- разложение на отрезке $[-l, l]$ $$f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{m=1}^\infty(a_m \cos\frac{m\pi x}{l} + b_m \sin\frac{m\pi x}{l})$$
- $$\begin{align}& a_m = \frac{1}{l}\int_{-l}^l f(u) \cos \frac{m\pi u}{l}\\ & b_m = \frac{1}{l}\int_{-l}^l f(u)\sin \frac{m\pi u}{l}du \end{align} m = 1, 2, 3, ...$$
- $$f(x) = \underset{= 0}{\underline{\frac{1}{2l} \int_{-l}^l f(u)du}} + \frac{1}{l}\sum_{m=1}^\infty \int_{-l}^l \cos(\frac{m\pi}{l}(u - x))f(u)du$$
- $$z_m = \frac{m\pi}{l} \to \Delta z_{m} = z_{m+1} - z_m = \frac{\pi}{l}$$
- $$\frac{1}{\pi} \sum_{m=1}^\infty \Delta z_{m-1} \int_{-l}^l f(u) \cos(z_m(u - x))du$$
- $$l \to \infty \quad f(x) = \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(u) \cos(z(u - x))du \quad \text{интеграл фурье}$$
- $$f(x) = \int_0^{\infty}[a(z)\cos(zx) + b(z)\sin(zx)]dz \quad \text{интегральная формула фурье}$$
- $$\begin{align}& a(z) = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\infty}^\infty f(u) \cos (zu)du\\ & b(z) = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\infty}^\infty f(u) \sin (zu)du\end{align}$$
